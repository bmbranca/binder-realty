Mr. Binder has been actively engaged in the real estate business in the Chicago Metropolitan area since 1977. Active as full time real estate appraiser from 1977 to present and Founder of Binder Realty Consultants, Inc., a real estate appraisal firm established in 1986. Active in the field of real estate since 1977, specializing in real estate appraisal and consulting. Employment background includes experience with mortgage bankers, corporations, financial institutions, and independent fee appraisers.
