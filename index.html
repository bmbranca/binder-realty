About Us

BINDER REALTY CONSULTANTS, INC. is a real estate appraisal organization specializing in consultation and appraisal assignments on all types of properties. Work has been completed on many property types within the Chicagoland Metropolitan area plus properties in the States of Indiana, Wisconsin and Iowa.

Experience

Active in the field of real estate since 1977, specializing in real estate appraisal and consulting. Employment background includes experience with mortgage bankers, corporations, attorneys, financial institutions, insurance companies and private owners and developers. Prior to the establishment of BINDER REALTY CONSULTANTS, INC., Mr. Binder was associated with two independent appraisal firms and a national appraisal firm specializing in commercial consultation and valuation work. Gordon S. Bandemer and James W. Vliek have 19 and 18 years of commercial appraisal experience. Gordon S. Bandemer has been employed with Binder Realty Consultants since 1986. James W. Vliek has been employed with Binder Realty Consultants since 1998.

Services

Binder Realty Consultants, Inc. has completed appraisals on various types of properties within the Chicagoland Metropolitan area plus properties in the States of Indiana, Wisconsin, Michigan and Iowa. We are experienced in most types of properties including:

  • apartment buildings
  • shopping centers
  • residential subdivisions
  • automotive dealerships
  • office buildings
  • industrial buildings
  • vacant land
  • condominium conversions
